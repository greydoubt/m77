Film #2


"Read this story."

"All right," Lester said as Kid Dracula handed him Pavel's screenplay.

"I need you to make a better version of this or I will kill you."

After taking a considerable amount of time to ponder the quandary he found himself in, Lester answered.

"I have a story for you."

This garnered no reaction from Kid Dracula, who just continued to stare at Lester.

"Let's make Old Liam fight the Master of Wolves in the Forest of Despair."

Of course, this was difficult for Lester to even say without laughing. His teeth clamped into the sides of his mouth. The name of the story reminded him of a death metal band album. This silently delighted him. He supposed that his captors would not find the humor in this, so he tried his best not to let out even the tiniest squeal.

"What happens in it?"

Lester bowed his head and closed his eyes to think. This only lasted a few minutes before Refren, the goon with the broadest-shoulders, hit him on KD's command.
