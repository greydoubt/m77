The beekeeper suit snagged on the thornberry bushes. The beekeeper himself let out a deep bellow because he knew he was fucked. He was eaten by a giant wolf and the top half of his carcass was dragged back by the wolf to her nest, to feed her cubs. Each cub had a water faucet where snoots should be. 
	The 18mm film tore apart in the loudly whirring projector. That would be the first and only time any audience got to see Lester Lane's newest piece.
