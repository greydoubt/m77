Two Soldiers -- one Irish, one Scottish -- serve together under HM. Upon returning to London, each explores his roots in different ways. The Scott becomes involved in the independence movement, which, upon its success, rapidly transforms Northern Britain into a petrol-monarchial nation almost overnight. But because Nova Nova Scotia (NNS) could not join NATO, the new crown seeks alignment with Russia -- forming a Northern Block.

His Irish friend fights his own demons as he tries to reason with his old friend, to come back. Their mutual friend from Wales observed all of this as always; he was used to being left aside but bitter his heart, he refused to be mocked or ripped asunder.
