After it was all done, Lester curled up into a ball with the last of his strength. He began to sob, his eyes the only part of his sorrowful husk not paralyzed by this night's visit.



After what may have been mere minutes or a few hours, his bedroom door creaked open. The sound took its time reaching Lester, but once it registered, the sudden rage of his father immediately followed. The size of his father's teeth always intimidated Lester.

"You little sack of shit. I know you took my heroin," his father yelled. His teeth click-clacked between accusations.

"Look at how strung out you are, you fuck."
