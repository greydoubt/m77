Tonight called for special attire. Lester Lane and Kandy, his sometimes girlfriend sometimes emotional pincushion, took turns working on his anti-social vest. At first, it was her idea, just a generic transplant from the early punk scene which she was too young to have seen or understood but still fascinated her. Ask any real punk and they'd scoff or laugh; she thought however that she was the real deal. 
	"Lester you are so punk," she said as she would often.
	
	Lester finished up the vest with that special touch that gave him all of his credibility. With a marker he made intricate designs that wouldn't garner a single remark from the average person, but would deeply offend that same person if they had even the slightest visual impairment.
