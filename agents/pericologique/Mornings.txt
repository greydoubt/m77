The sun came up and poked through the cold. It failed to wake Lester but scared away the rat that had spent much of the night licking the salty, sticky taste of Kandy's mascara imbued tears. The rat had no idea what made Lester's girlfriend cry or why she still curled up around him. Eventually the rat would scurry into the crevice it had chewed in the wall, and sleep the day away with its own family. Lester spent most of his days in the manner as did the rats, asleep and unaware.
	Meanwhile, in Hollywood, two hours on the clock prior, Fred had just returned home from tea and crumpets with a high-powered movie exec.
