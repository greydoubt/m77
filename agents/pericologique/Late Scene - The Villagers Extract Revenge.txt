Gathered on the hills and thirsty to rush and drain the parasitic Dragos, the lord that had ruled over them for decades, the villagers waited for the mansion's iron gate to open at last. Dawn rolled by with nary a sign.

One held a torch. His old body bore the signs of a hard life, he was only 40 years old, but in the time he had spent in Cluj, he had seen everything come and go. The winter had many times erased his family's prospects but that never compelled him to give up hope. [But Dragos ... did.]

They spoke amongst themselves.
